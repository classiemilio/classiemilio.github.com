TimeTreble

TimeTreble is Visualization for the full history of Billboard Hot 100 data. It uses D3.js to visualize the data in a record for a specific year with different layers for genre, artist, and song. Clicking on an item in the song layer plays the song. User can cycle across the years of documented popular music. An auditory trip down memory lane!

Premonit

A startup I co-founded, Premonit was a social platform where users predictes on the outcomes of future events. Users could predict who's more likely to win a sports game, where political candidates will stand in the polls, etc. The goal was to aggregate users’ predictions to effectively predict future outcomes.
